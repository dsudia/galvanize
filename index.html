Cities I've Lived In

I was born in Oakland. When I was three, my mother moved to Lafayette and my dad followed her. As a teenager my family moved to Pleasanton. When I moved out on my own I moved to Alameda, and then to Walnut Creek when I met my wife. My wife and I moved to Oakland, then Phoenix, Boulder and finally Denver, where we live now.
